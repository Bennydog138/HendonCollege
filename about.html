Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To inspire lifelong learning, creativity, and ethical values in every child.

Our Mission

Provide a nurturing environment that balances academics, arts, sports, and community service.

School History

Established in 2025, Hendon College has grown into a thriving institution with a strong reputation for quality education, nurturing teachers, and innovative learning programs. Over the years, thousands of students have flourished in our care.
